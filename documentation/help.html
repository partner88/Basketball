How to Use

This game was designed to work on desktop and mobile devices. 

To play the game, you simply have to click the screen to throw a ball. You get 60 seconds to try to make as many balls as possible. When you run out of time, your score will be displayed.

To add the game to your website, simply upload the index.html file, and the src and res folder to your server. No other setup is required.
